How To Use

The app is divided into 2 pages. On the first page tap on the Generate button & try to identify the Avesta letter before the answer is shown. On the second page you can test your word skills by playing the game wherein you choose the correct alphabets to form the word/s shown in English. Switch between the 2 pages by tapping on the top left corner button.


While playing the game on the 2nd page if you get stuck, you can tap on the "!" button (bottom right corner) to reveal the correct answer.


For an optimum experience, install the app by tapping on "Add to Home Screen" in your broswer. (for Safari on iOS devices, tap on Share > Add to Home Screen)


Remember to update the app (by pulling down the page) from time to time to get the latest changes.


Happy Learning! :)
